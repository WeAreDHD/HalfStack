ABOUT US

LaunchX empowers students to change the world, equipping them with the skills and mindset to start a company—regardless of age.

Our programs help students leverage their talents and tenacity to build a viable startup. We’ll train you in the skills and innovative thinking that allow founders to create successful businesses, challenging you to take a real startup from idea to execution and impact.

OUR STORY

We believe that age is just a number.

As a young girl our founder, Laurie Stach, always knew she wanted to run a company. Teachers and adults validated her ambitions, assuring her that she would be successful—someday. Little Laurie accepted that she was too young—she would wait for “someday”—and she tucked that dream away.

Years later, grown-up Laurie decided the day had finally come. She decided to turn her jewelry-making hobby into a full-fledged business. Laurie crowdfunded her start-up costs, built a basic website, and eventually made her very first sale.

The experience inspired an even better idea. Laurie realized that it was the most impactful learning experience she’d ever had—teaching hard skills like prototyping and financials, as well as lessons in problem-solving, initiative, and grit.

What’s more, she realized Little Laurie did not have to wait to pursue that dream. As a high school student, Laurie had all of the curiosity and optimism she needed.

And so, LaunchX was born.

At LaunchX, we believe entrepreneurial education is the best way to create college and career readiness—promoting student ownership and engagement in the process. We believe the adaptability required of entrepreneurs is imperative in today’s rapidly changing job market.

Most of all, we believe that high school students are capable of making a real and meaningful impact, now. And we’re here to help them do it.
